\section*{Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors}
For a matrix $A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}$, we define
\begin{itemize}
	\item an \textbf{eigenvalue} $\lambda \in \mathbb{R}$, and
	\item an \textbf{eigenvector} $x \in \mathbb{R}^n \setminus 0$ such that 
	\item $Ax = \lambda x$
\end{itemize}
